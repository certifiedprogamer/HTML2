Game Reviews

R.E.P.O

Steam page

Description:

R.E.P.O is a new co-op horror game with up to six players where you and your gang of goons break into houses and steal everything that isnt nailed down whilst avoiding monsters (the homeowners) in order to appease your creator, the taxman. Very fun with stupid friends so i give it a ★8/10.

Things I like

  • Every monster can be killed one way or another, and the ingame shop that you go to after every level always supplies something to help you with that
  • There are multiple ways to revive friends if they happen to die during a level.
  • You can buy a shotgun

Things I don't like

  • Some of the bigger objects crumble into dust instantly when they hit the smallest bump, losing all their value
  • Weapons like the frying pan need to be recharged after enough use and dont do enough damage to be worth their price or the risk taken by using them.

HELLDIVERS 2

steam page

Description:

HELLDIVERS 2 is a co-op third person shooter that can be played with up to 4 players. Your job as a helldiver is to save freedom and democracy from the clutches of tyrrany with a large variety of weapons and stratagems at your disposal. I give it a strong ★9/10. Very fun.

Things I like

  • You can nuke bugs and communist robots with your friends
  • Large variety of armors and loadouts

Things I don't like

  • The backpack drone stratagem can sometimes shoot you in the head when trying to shoot enemies, killing you instantly.

Trepang2

steam page

Description:

Trepang2 is a fast-paced action-packed FPS with some horror elements. With a variety of abilities at your disposal, you can shred your enemies with a hail of bullets, perform swift executions in close quarters, and do stylish slow-motion kills. Perfect ★10/10 game.

Things I like

  • Good graphics.
  • All main missions (along with the DLC missions) are very fun and well made.
  • Large amount of weapons allowing for many different playstyles.

Things I don't like

  • None.
